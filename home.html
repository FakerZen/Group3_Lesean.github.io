ABOUT US

Established in 2022 as a Lifelabs project, a subject in APEC Schools, We, Group 3, composed of 5 members, are committed to push this brand till the end of the quarter, or the end of the School Year. We offer multiple products, such as our Aenon Water Drinking Bottles, both purified and mineral, and our Tumblers, offered in multiple colors.

OUR MISSION

Our Brand, "Aenon" is commited to help people to bring clean and accessible drinking water across the world.

OUR LOGO & TAGLINE

Our logo for our brand was inspired by a water droplet. This was our first and is our current logo design of our brand. Our Tagline, "Drink to be Alive" is there to remind our audience and customers to stay hydrated to be alive.

OUR INTRO VIDEO

"Short and Simple". This is what we're aiming for our introduction video.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is the source of your water?

Our water is sourced from a natural underground aquifer, which is regularly tested to ensure it meets all safety and purity standards.

Is your water bottled in a facility that follows proper sanitation protocols?

Yes, our bottling facility follows strict sanitation protocols to ensure the quality and safety of our water.

Do you offer delivery service?

Yes, we offer delivery service to both residential and commercial customers.
